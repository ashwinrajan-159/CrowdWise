## Honest findings
- **Analogs add no point-prediction lift on this data.** The GBM already learns from the same drivers (cause/hour/corridor), so any positive analog weight monotonically raised MAE. Analogs are retained as the *explanation + uncertainty surface* (auditable 'here are the 5 events behind this' + spread-based confidence), which is their real job. Blend weight capped at 0.10.
- **Real duration is heavily censored** (~37% observed) and long-tailed (median ~54 min, p90 ~12 h). MAE on it is large and honestly reported; the synthetic target exists precisely so the full north-star loop is demonstrable before a real probe feed.
- **No speed/volume in the source** — north-star delay-hours use the duration/synthetic outcome × exposure as a proxy. A connected-vehicle feed drops in as a third `TargetProvider` with no downstream change.
