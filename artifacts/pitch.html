What we don't hide

A pitch that buries its weak spots isn't credible. Phase 0 exists to find these cheaply.

Honest findings
  • Forecast accuracy is a diagnostic, not the goal. Nobody in a control room cares about RMSE — they care about clearance time and delay-hours. Our KPIs are ranked accordingly.
  • Historical analogs add no prediction lift here — the model already learns the same signal. So we keep them for what they're good at: the auditable "5 past events behind this call."
  • The +23% is a replay counterfactual, not a field trial. A real connected-vehicle feed drops into the same interface to make it real — the seam is already built.
